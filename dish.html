Tamilok in Palawan

The Tamilok is a popular Filipino delicacy also known as ‘woodworm’ because it is found in
decaying, rotten logs or thick tree trunks submerged underwater in swamps-like mangroves.

Tukslob Buwa in
Cebu City

The Tuslob buwa is a popular Filipino street food found in the Pasil neighborhood of Cebu
City.Its chief ingredients are pork liver and brain, which get sautéed with garlic, onions, shrimp paste, and chilis.

Ant Egg in Ilocos

The Ant eggs refer to both the eggs and pupae of weaver ants eaten in several countries across Southeast Asia, especially Laos and Northeastern Thailand (Isan).
